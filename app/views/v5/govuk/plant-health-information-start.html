Before you start

You'll need the name of the plant, plant product, pest or disease to find plant health information.

You can use:

  • the scientific name or any synonyms
  • a common name in some cases​

This service:

  • does not provide trade information about exporting plants or plant products
  • is not a pest or disease identification service​
  • does not have information about pests and diseases native to Great Britain

Get help with this service

If you have any queries or you need technical support, email planthealth.info@apha.gov.uk
