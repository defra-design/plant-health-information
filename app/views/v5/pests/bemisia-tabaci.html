Reporting suspicions of Bemisia tabaci

You only need to report sightings or suspicions of Bemisia tabaci if found on certain plants in certain formats. These are listed at the bottom of the page. ​ You do not need to report this pest if you find it on other plant-format combinations. 

If Bemisia tabaci is found on any of the listed plant-format combinations during routine plant health inspections, you will be asked to take action.
