Ips typographus

Preferred name

  • Ips typographus

Common names

  • Eight-spined engraver
  • Eight-toothed spruce bark beetle
  • Larger eight-toothed European spruce bark beetle
  • Spruce bark beetle

Synonyms

  • Bostrichus octodentatus
  • Dermestes typographus
