Pest or disease

Anoplophora glabripennis

! Warning You must report suspicions of Anoplophora glabripennis if found in certain areas of Great Britain.

For details of these areas, check Annex 3 of the Plant Health Regulations 2020.

To report suspicions, you must contact the relevant authority.
